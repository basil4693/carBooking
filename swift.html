Performance

The Maruti Suzuki Swift offers impressive performance for its price segment. With a top speed of 170 km/h, it provides a good blend of power and fuel efficiency. The 0-100 km/h acceleration is estimated at around 14 seconds.

Fuel Efficiency

The Swift achieves an impressive fuel efficiency of around 23.4 km/l (ARAI Mumbai test). This translates to an impressive range of up to 480 km on a single tank of fuel.

Interior and Exterior Design

The Swift offers a contemporary and dynamic design with a blend of sharp lines and flowing curves. The dashboard is neatly organized and equipped with dual climate control systems. The rear seating offers good legroom and headroom. The exterior design also features sleek and sporty alloys, LED tail lamps, and 13-inch alloy wheels.

Safety Features

The Swift is equipped with several safety features to ensure the safety of the driver and passengers. These include dual front airbags, anti-lock braking system (ABS), electronic brakeforce distribution (EBD), and driver-side and front passenger-side side-impact airbags.
