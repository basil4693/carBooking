Tata Nexon - Performance

The Tata Nexon offers impressive performance for its price segment. With a top speed of 182 km/h, it provides a good blend of power and fuel efficiency. The 0-100 km/h acceleration is estimated at around 10 seconds.

Fuel Efficiency

The Nexon achieves an impressive fuel efficiency of around 22.1 km/l (ARAI Mumbai test). This translates to an impressive range of up to 515 km on a single tank of fuel.

Interior and Exterior Design

The Nexon offers a modern and dynamic design with a blend of sharp lines and flowing curves. The dashboard is neatly organized and equipped with dual climate control systems. The rear seating offers good legroom and headroom. The exterior design also features sleek and sporty alloys, LED tail lamps, and 15-inch alloy wheels.

Safety Features

The Nexon is equipped with several safety features to ensure the safety of the driver and passengers. These include dual front airbags, anti-lock braking system (ABS), electronic brakeforce distribution (EBD), and driver-side and front passenger-side side-impact airbags. Additionally, it features electronic stability control (ESC) for added safety.
